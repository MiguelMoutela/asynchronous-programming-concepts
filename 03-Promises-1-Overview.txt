
States
======

           +---> Resolved/Fulfilled
           |
Pending ---+
           |
           +---> Rejected

API
===

CREATE:
p = new Promise((resolve, reject) => { ... })
Promise.resolve(value)
Promise.reject(err)

USE:
p.then(onSuccess[, onError]): Promise

COMBINE:
Promise.all ([ p1, p2, ... ]): Promise
Promise.race([ p1, p2, ... ]): Promise
